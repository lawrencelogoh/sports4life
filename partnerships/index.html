Collaborating for Impact

At Sports 4 Life Ministries, we believe in the power of collaboration. Our partnerships with NGOs, donors, and supporters have been instrumental in expanding our reach and impact.

Here are some of our valued partners:

1. Fellowship of Christian Athletes (FCA):

  • Location: 8701 Leeds Road, Kansas City, MO, USA
  • Affiliation: We are proud to be affiliated with FCA, an organization that shares our commitment to empowering youth through sports and faith.

2. Hoops for Hope International:

  • Location: 2911 Herring Avenue, Suite 200, Waco, TX 76708
  • Email: hoopsforhopeinternational@gmail.com
  • Phone: 254-340-0441
  • Collaboration: We have a strong partnership with Hoops for Hope International, working together to make a positive impact on youth and communities.

3. Baylor University, Waco, TX (Unofficial Partnership):

  • Our unofficial partnership with Baylor University, Waco, TX, allows us to tap into educational resources and expertise, further enhancing our programs.

These partnerships are a testament to our commitment to holistic youth development. Together, we aim to create a lasting impact on the lives of young individuals and communities.
