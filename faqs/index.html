Your Questions Answered

Here are answers to some frequently asked questions about Sports 4 Life Ministries and how you can get involved in our mission:

Q1: What is the mission of Sports 4 Life Ministries?

  • A: Our mission is to create a safe space for the holistic development of youth through sports, education, and faith-based programs.

Q2: How can I get involved as a volunteer?

  • A: We welcome volunteers who share our passion for empowering youth. Please reach out to us through our contact information to explore volunteering opportunities.

Q3: Can I make a one-time donation to support your initiatives?

  • A: Yes, you can make a one-time donation to support our programs. Visit our secure donation portal for more information.

Q4: Are my donations tax-deductible?

  • A: We are a registered nonprofit organization. Donations made to Sports 4 Life Ministries may be tax-deductible in accordance with your local tax laws. Please consult your tax advisor for specific details.

Q5: How do I stay updated on your projects and events?

  • A: You can stay informed about our projects, events, and news by exploring our website and following us on social media.

Q6: Are there partnership opportunities available for organizations?

  • A: Yes, we believe in the power of collaboration. If your organization shares our vision for community development, please contact us to explore partnership possibilities.

Q7: What are the core values of Sports 4 Life Ministries?

  • A: Our core values are JOY: Jesus first, Others, Yourself. These values guide our interactions and relationships.

We hope these FAQs provide clarity on our organization and how you can contribute to our mission of empowering youth and communities.
